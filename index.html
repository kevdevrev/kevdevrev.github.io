About Me

I am a first generation latino computer science student at the University of Central Florida. I was born in Colombia, and came to the United States at the age of 3. I am everyday grateful for what this country has provided for me and the opportunities that are still to come.

I currently work in the ISUE lab with my mentor Dr. LaViola Jr, doing research involving machine learning as well as virtual reality. I have a 3.7 overall GPA, and a 3.9 in-major GPA.

My biggest accomplishments include having been awarded the Student Excellence in Leadership Award from Valencia College, and becoming a member of the McNair family Road To PhD program.

My favorite things to do include but are not limited to: feeding my betta fish, hiking, spoiling my birds, and watching anime.
